About Me


"Life is a symphony, and my passions are the notes that harmonize it. From the rhythm of soccer to the melody of music, the adventure of travel to the savory taste of good food, I find endless enjoyment in the simple yet beautiful things that make life worth living."

Hailing from Kerala and having grown up in the Middle East, I have completed by bachelors of commerce from Christ University. I have 2 years of experience in audit and assurance after which I worked as a business analyst with hands on experience primarily in ELK stack. Presently, I am in San Francisco chasing my Analytics dream! I am an avid soccer fan and love to play FIFA on a regular basis. I listen to alot of music and would love to jam!

I aspire to work in Data Science projects and harness the power of analytical technologies,statistical reasoning and business decision making to create innovative solutions that drive positive change for my organization.
